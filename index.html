About

Imperial Donuts is an independently owned and family run business that specializes in the creation of artisan donuts. Eastablished in early 2014, we have been blown away by the undexpected success and popularity of our carefylly crafted donuts. Our donuts are freshly prepared each day prior to sun-rise and amongst our most popular donuts include the honey-glazed donut, the jam filled-donu and our iced-glazed donut.

Currenlty we make 117 different types of donuts on a daily-basis and have two stores in the Toronto area, one store in Montreal and one store in Chicago. Definitely pop by one of our locations to try at least one of our donuts but if you can't make it - dont worry! We've recently introduced both a catering service for those special occasions such as weddings & birthday's and have also recently introduced a delivery service for out 'Surprise Donut Collection' box which contains 6 different donut flavours.
